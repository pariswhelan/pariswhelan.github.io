Hawaii Snorkelling Cruise

  • Molokai
  • Lana'i
  • Olowalu
  • Honomalino Bay
  • Kailua Akona

Packages Include

Price
$999 per person

Accommodation
6 nights aboard a 36-guest boutique yacht in large queen sized cabins

Food
All meals onboard are catered for.

Activities
All activities in the itinerary are included.

Day 1 → Molokai
The first day begins by being welcomed aboard and familiarising yourself with your new home for the week. Enjoy glasses of champagne as you wave away the shore and finish the day by an early dinner party before the next day begins.

Day 2 → Molokai
Be greeted by the sunrise for an early start in Molokai. Head into the local township to meet with locals and dive into the true Hawaiian culture by hiking up to a picturesque waterfall towering in Halawa’s cathedral valley. Check out the local museum before enjoying an evening feast accompanied by a traditional Hawaiian performance.

Day 3 → Lana'i
Visit the volcanic private island of Lana’i and explore it’s diverse marine life. Choose between snorkelling, paddle boarding or kayaking to make your way around this hidden gem. Learn about ancient legends of Hawaiian princesses as you hike up to Sweetheart Rock and watch the ocean from a different angle.

Day 4 → Olowalu
If you want to search for dolphins, whales and other marine life then Day 4 in Olowalu is perfect for you. Snorkel your way through the crystal clear water and get to know the coral landscape. Situated in a sea turtle habitat, you are guaranteed to come across all sorts of underwater fascinations.

Day 5 → Honomalino Bay
Head into Honomalino Bay and marvel at the white beaches fringed with coconut palms. Spend the day in the water amongst the coral or take a hike up to the volcanic mountains. However you choose to spend the day, be sure to end it by night snorkelling with Giant Pacific Manta Rays.

Day 6 → Kailua-Akona
Take a turn south and explore the untouched shores of Kailua-Akona. Take the day at your own leisure and stroll through the historic yet charming township. Be sure to reflect on your adventurous week at the final dinner aboard the cruise before departing back to the mainland.
